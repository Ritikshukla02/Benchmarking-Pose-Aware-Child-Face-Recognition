# Limitations and Future Work

---

## Limitations

While the system achieved strong performance, several constraints exist:

- Small sample size (27 subjects)
- Controlled indoor environment
- Manual pose structuring via filename
- No automatic pose estimation module
- Assumes reliable face detection performance

Results may vary under real-world surveillance conditions.

---

## Future Improvements

Future research directions include:

- Expanding dataset size and diversity
- Integrating automatic pose detection
- Testing under real-world lighting variations
- Deploying in live camera environments

Further benchmarking with larger-scale child datasets would strengthen generalization analysis.
